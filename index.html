Welcome to MindTrack!

MindTrack is a mobile app designed to help users monitor their mood, stress levels, and daily habits.

Our goal is to improve mental health awareness through simple daily check-ins and insights.

Accessible, personalized self-care: A digital tool that supports mental health by helping users track and manage what matters most to them.

Holistic tracking for awareness: Monitor moods, habits, and overall wellbeing to build insight and encourage positive routines over time.

Evidence-informed engagement: Early research suggests digital mental health platforms can boost self-care engagement by up to 45% versus traditional methods.
